The task of the stakeholder identification agent is to take a draft proposal for a pay-for-success or impact bond and:

1. Identify the specific stakeholders that wish to be included in the proposal, namely the government and the target impact investors.
2. Specifically define the target population and the target metrics or KPIs.
3. Suggest the specific financial terms and mechanisms which will be used in the operation of the pay-for-success instrument or bond.
